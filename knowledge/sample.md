# YourCompany Policies

## Refunds and Returns
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase if they are not satisfied with the product. To request a refund, they must provide their order number and the email address used for the purchase.

## Shipping
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping is available for an additional fee and takes 1-2 business days.

## Account Issues
If a customer cannot log in, tell them to visit `https://yourcompany.com/forgot-password` to reset their password. For locked accounts, they must contact a human agent.

## Business Hours
Our support team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM EST.

## Contact
If an issue cannot be resolved by the AI, please escalate the ticket to the human support queue. Customers can also email `support@yourcompany.com`.
